About Me

Who am I?

My name is Angel Juarez. I like to describe myself as an always excited, shy, and accidentally blunt person. I always do my best to do good. Good work and good things for other people. Sometimes I might stray but I think that's ok. I always find my way to become a more sincere and compassionate person.

What do I do?

I am a full stack web developer. I work with HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Postgres, and Sequelize. I learned all of these skills by attending the codebridge program with Per Scholas and then going on to attend the Web Development Immersive course with General Assembly.
